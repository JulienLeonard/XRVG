Module XRVG::Splittable
In: lib/samplation.rb

Splittable module, based on FloatFunctor

Concept

Basically allows advanced subdivision computations from an continuous "Interval" object.

Example

 (1.0..2.0).splits(2) => [(1.0..1.5), (1.5..2.0)]
