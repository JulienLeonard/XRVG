Principle

One seed: two circles Then two rules:

  • compute tangent circles to current circle pair
  • keep only those who do not intersect previous computed circles

Parameters:

  • seed circles position and relative radius
  • radiuses and side of new tangent circles relative to their parents

Examples

Seeds equal and tangents Radius Rule: take big circle radius Side rule: both

Conclusion: not very interesting
